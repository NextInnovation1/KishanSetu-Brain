02 · Farmer flow · identical on Android (Compose) & iOS (SwiftUI)

Two taps to sell, one glance to trust

Designed for low digital literacy: max two actions per screen, picture grids instead of dropdowns, every price shown against the mandi rate it beats.

03 · Buyer flow · identical on Android (Compose) & iOS (SwiftUI)

Order by grade, verify by farm

Professional and restrained for HoReCa managers. Grade chips everywhere produce appears; traceability is the premium moment — a vertical trace from farm to door with timestamps.

04 · Website · vanilla JS, EN/हिं toggle

The broken chain is the centerpiece

Single page for credibility in sales meetings and for lead capture. The hero states the promise; the chain diagram shows exactly whose cut we remove; the form feeds straight into the backend's /leads table.
